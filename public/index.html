Movement & camera

[wait N]
pause N seconds
[walk to N]
glide to N% of stage width
[enter from left|right]
walk on from offstage
[exit left|right]
walk off that side
[look left|right|up|down|front]
gaze direction
[view face|body]
camera close-up / full frame
[emote name]
alias for a held-pose action
[wave] [jump] …
any action name from the manifest

Scene & frames

[layout preset]
single|split|thirds|pip-tr|tl|br|bl
[frame id key:val …]
slot|bg|character|view|facing|rect:x,y,w,h
[frame id clear]
remove a frame (never the last one)
[scene bg]
set active frame's background
[frameId direction]
target that frame, e.g. [left wave]

Content & overlays

[show image:id]
content tile; fit:contain|cover
[lower-third "a" "b" hold:ms]
title bar (default hold 6000, hold:0 = persist)
[lower-third clear]
dismiss it early
[captions on|off]
subtitle bar for spoken lines
[iris|fade in|out ms]
fullscreen take (default 700ms)

Audio

[music id]
crossfade to a looping track
[music off]
fade out music
[sfx id]
one-shot sound effect

Cast & props

[place what at x scale:s]
place a prop/character actor (id: behind: optional)
[id move N / scale N]
glide / resize a prop actor
[id spin] [id bounce]
prop actor flourishes
[wear target prop]
pin a prop to an anchor (default head)
[unwear target]
remove the worn prop
[remove id]
destroy a placed actor
[clear]
wipe actors, worn props, content & overlays
